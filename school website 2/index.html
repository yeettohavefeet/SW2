The Brigade School

Hi!

Located within the prestigious Brigade Gateway enclave in Malleswaram - Rajajinagar, Northwest Bangalore, the Brigade School in Malleswaram has a unique ambience. In nine years the school has become a much sought after institution of academic excellence that ensures the all-round development of the child. The school takes great care to ensure that academics and co-curricular activities are well balanced. Classes start at the Nursery level up to Standard 8. The graduating batch of class 10 has achieved 100% first class results.

At The Brigade Schools, we encourage independent thinking and co-curricular activities while keeping the needs of a structured education firmly in mind. Our education system, equips every student with the necessary skill sets to take his/her place anywhere in the world. The facilities and excellent team of facilitators help the students develop positive relationships and the opportunity to realize their potential to the fullest. We provide an exceptionally caring and learning-focused environment for our students.

About Us

The Brigade Schools have earned the reputation of being institutions of all round excellence. Our three schools at JP Nagar, Mahadevapura and Malleswaram, give the children a head start with the benefits of a unique, Montessori based pre-primary education. Our exceptional curriculum, along with co-curricular activities is designed with extra care. Our schools have been instituted by Brigade Foundation as part of its interest in Education, Health and Community Service.

The aim is to provide an International level of education, with a number of activities but well within city limits. Our strong teaching staff, with supportive parents and enthusiastic students contribute to the success of the school. Our students have developed into confident young adults with a strong sense of awareness. In our three schools we recognize that all students are uniquely talented. The schools provide opportunities for children to do whatever they aspire to do - they could be future leaders of the country, get into premier institutions and follow their dreams!

Co-Curricular

Sport continues to be taken seriously with specialized coaches for Basketball, Football, Athletics and Volleyball. The timetable and sports facilities allow each and every child to excel in these activities.They are assigned to different houses and compete, thus inculcating team spirit.

Sports and swimming are an integral part of our curriculum.The schools have a robust Fine Art, Performing Art and Dance program. It is a stress buster as well as an opportunity to discover talents that can be taken forward.

"Mind space" is an activity unique to our school where children put into practice what they have learnt and "learn by doing".Frequent performances on stage and opportunities for public speaking give the children an added boost of confidence.
